CLIMBER

My passion is climbing; especially indoor competition on lead. I have been a GB Lead team member for a total of 6 years, both as a Junior and a Senior. Winner of the British Lead Climbing Championship for 5 consecutive years in relevant age groups.

SPONSORS

ABOUT ME

My other passion is my Masters at The University of Sheffield where I am studying Aerospace Engineering, my key focus is in programming and flight control systems.

I enjoy training and pushing myself, finding it a great time for thinking. As well as lead climbing I have started to compete at bouldering, with some success. I now plan to tackle some serious outdoor climbing.

China – World Universities Climbing Competition

Firstly a massive thank you to Sports Sheffield, for organising the funding and apologies for taking so long getting the blog out. The plan was hatched after the success of Sheffield University at the European Championships to put a GB team in for The Universities World Championships. With the help of Steve from Sheffield Sport, the BUCS organisation and the BMC a team was selected being Jen Wood, Luke Dawson, Billy Ridal, myself and team manager Steve from Sheffield Sport...
